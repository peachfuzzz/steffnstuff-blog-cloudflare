## Who am I? 

Heya, I’m Stephen. You can call me Stephen (Curry, not Hawking), or Steph, or Peachfuzzz. I’m a new grad with experience primarily in data science, and I’ve been actively interested in game design for years.

## What is this blog about?

I write about things for which I have strong feelings. These things tend to center around games, but I’m not opposed to writing beyond games. I particularly enjoy the formalization and teaching of games, two things on which I spend a lot of time.
